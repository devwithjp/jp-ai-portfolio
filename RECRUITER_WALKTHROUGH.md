# Recruiter Walkthrough

A no-jargon tour for a non-technical recruiter or hiring manager.

## The one-liner
JP builds AI products end-to-end — he can define what to build (like a product manager) **and** build, test, and ship it (like an engineer). This portfolio proves both with four working apps.

## What to click
1. **Home** — the positioning and the four projects.
2. **Any project → "Case study"** — switch the tabs:
   - **Product** = the PM thinking (who it's for, what's in/out, how success is measured).
   - **Engineering** = how it's built.
   - **Demo / Metrics / Learnings** = proof and takeaways.
3. **Open a live demo** — every one works instantly, no signup, no keys.

## What each project shows (plain English)
- **AgentEval Studio** — tells you whether an AI feature is *good enough to ship*, with a clear ship/hold call.
- **SignalDesk AI** — turns a pile of customer feedback into a prioritised plan, with every claim backed by a real quote.
- **ScreenSense QA** — reviews a screenshot of a screen and lists the UX/accessibility problems by severity, with fixes.
- **WorkflowPilot** — an AI assistant that proposes actions but **asks permission before doing anything**, and logs everything.

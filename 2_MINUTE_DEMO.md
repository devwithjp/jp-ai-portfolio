# 2-Minute Demo Script

Goal: prove range (engineering + product) fast.

1. **Open the portfolio home (15s).** "I'm an AI Product Engineer — I build, evaluate, and ship AI products. Here are four, each with a working demo and both an engineering and a product case study. Everything runs mock-first, so no keys needed."
2. **AgentEval Studio (35s).** Open a sample suite → **Run eval**. "Deterministic checks first, then an LLM-as-judge. The output isn't a wall of scores — it's a release-gate recommendation. The improved prompt variant beats the baseline, and it tells me whether to ship."
3. **SignalDesk AI (35s).** Load sample feedback → **Analyze** → open a theme → **Generate PRD**. "RAG over feedback: it clusters pain points, scores opportunity, and writes a PRD where every claim is cited — guardrail is at least two sources."
4. **WorkflowPilot (30s).** Propose a plan → show the approval queue → uncheck a tool → execute. "A safe agent: nothing runs until I approve, and an unauthorized tool is blocked and logged. Trust is the product."
5. **Close (5s).** "Each project has a PRD, metrics, and a case study — happy to go deep on any one."
